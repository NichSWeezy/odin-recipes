Ramen


Description

Impress your friends and family with this simple to make dish. Inspired by struggle and poverty, this recipe delivers an artificial, yet somewhat realistic flavor of chicken broth, combined with a pleasantly crunchy textured noodle. Enjoy the savior notes of umami as the MSG floods your tongue. You will be begging for more but there is only like 8 bites maximum in a serving.


Ingredients

  • 1 Murachen Ramen noodle cube
  • 1 Packet of seasoning
  • Water
