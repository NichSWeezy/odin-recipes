Butter Sandwich



Description

Do you like Texas Roadhouse rolls? Well this next recipe is nothing like that. This is what poverty tastes like.

Ingredients

  • 2x Slices of bread. Use the "Sandwich" bread from the Hotdog recipe.
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Put your slices of bread on a plate.
  2. Using a spoon, scoop butter out of the tub or off of the stick, and spread on bread.
  3. Enjoy.
